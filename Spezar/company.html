Spezar tech’s aim is to engage and build customized innovative solutions that can reduce factual difficulties and elevate scientific possibilities. Building and creating machines, robots, and automated structures to solve the existing challenges is our primary goal.

About the Founder
Riyasdeen Samsudeen, a 20 y/o entrepreneur, Founder, and CEO of Spezar tech Pvt. Ltd. started this venture with a common motive in his mind “Soldering Technology with Inaccessible”. He has experience of 5+ years in the field of Robotics & Automation and Embedded systems. He is also specialized in IoT, 3D printing technologies, 3D modeling & designing, Product development, Space technologies, and related domains. He has also trained and mentored a lot of young students.
